    Together we can.

    ASOJEBU is an association created by young students from Canada and Burundi to support compatriots who are in need. Thus, to integrate easily into society and participate in their own development and that of the country.

    Get Started

    Youth education

    ASOJEBU is located in Burundi in the region of East Africa. The association pays the school fees for these young people, offers financial support to destitute families and ensures the professional integration of these young people after their schooling.

    Women empowerment

    Facilitate the integration of young people and women in socio-economic development. Promote the empowerment of women to meet their needs and those of their country.

    Our Mission

    Contribute to the educational development of poor young people in Burundi. Facilitate the integration of young people and women in socio-economic development. Promote the empowerment of women to meet their needs and those of their country.

    Our Plan

    provide essential tools to all the youth in order to improve their education. Contribute towards the «Zero hunger» concept so that kids achieve the best academic performance. Start awareness programs in order to let all the women know their rights and values.

    Our Vision

    We believe in society where youth have access to school and education. Where gender has no difference and where women know theirs rights and values and able to support themselves or their families.
